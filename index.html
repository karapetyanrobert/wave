What is EIF

Enterprise Incubator Foundation (EIF) is one of the largest technology business incubators and IT/High-Tech development agencies in the region, operating in Armenia. Established in 2002 within the framework of the World Bank’s “Enterprise Incubator” project, we are called to support the development of information and communication technology sector in Armenia through creating a productive environment for innovation, technological advancement and company growth.

Our activities cover every aspect of sector development: ICT-related legal, business and educational reforms, investment channeling and creation of funding schemes for startups, individualized services and consulting for IT and Engineering companies, talent identification and workforce development. We act as a cross-point for all entities in the sector - public and private institutions, international organizations and government agencies, major multinationals and small startups and bring them together to act jointly towards the ultimate goal of ICT/High-Tech excellence.

"Wave" Youth Initiative of EIF

"Wave" is the youth initiative of Enterprise Incubator Foundation, which aims to form a conscious generation that makes decisions and generates changes, the one that lives and creates in Armenia of its dreams.

The goal of the initiative is to identify the potential and interests of young people aged 12 to 29 years from all regions of the Republic of Armenia and Yerevan and to create environments (in the formats of clubs, virtual discussions and interactive meetings) for self-organization and self-expression.

The initiative offers young people to join clubs covering various fields aimed at developing skills and abilities, make new acquaintances from different regions of the Republic of Armenia, get volunteering experience, lead and share their best experience.

By joining and getting on this incredible journey, Wave participants will embark on their own personal development route, which involves learning, performing, sharing and leading stages to develop skills and knowledge.

Along with all that, young people, being the bearers of the most important values, such as honesty and justice, courage and initiative, persistence, responsibility and caring, will spread these values in their communities by forming clubs and carrying out activities that are directly related to the scope of their interests. They can include various fields: technology, culture, sports, art, science, etc.

The "Wave" youth initiative is implemented with the support of "Armenia Workforce Development Activity" coordinated by Enterprise Incubator Foundation.

VANADZOR TECHNOLOGY CENTER

Vanadzor Technology Center (VTC) was established by the Enterprise Incubator Foundation with the support of Government of Armenia and the World Bank.

The mission of Vanadzor Technology Center is to turn Vanadzor into a regional and international center of information and high technologies by creating such a developing environment of opportunities and services that will contribute to the creation of new jobs and will support the talented young people and organizations to implement innovative business ideas contributing to the sustainable development of the region.
